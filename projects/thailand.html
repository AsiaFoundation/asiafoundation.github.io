Data-sharing for disaster risk management

The Asia Foundation and its partners in Thailand are applying modern, collaborative software concepts to official disaster alert systems. By ensuring alerts are machine-readable and shared openly, citizens get precise and actionable information in formats that are easy to understand and pass on to others.

In parallel, the Foundation is helping to convene an Open Data Working Group for disaster risk management – a neutral space for Thai stakeholders to come together and productively discuss the issues at hand, share technical lessons, and define best practices. This emerging community of partners will pilot data sharing and analysis technologies that help disaster risk management agencies protect property and save lives.
