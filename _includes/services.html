How We Work

Access to Information

Smartphones are now connecting millions of people in Asia. We’re leveraging this critical link to connect citizens with a global ecosystem of information and services.

Data & Analytics

We’re promoting new tools that help Asia’s policy makers, civic activists, and small business leaders rapidly collect and analyze a range of data previously unavailable to them.

Collaboration

We’ve integrated social media tools into our programs to connect our partners more fully, and promote diverse, grassroots networks capable of working in new ways.
